About the project

The goal of this project is to learn how to use bootstrap by building a multi-page website. The content of the site is a detailed resume of a collection of videos on the story of the internet and its surrounding technologies. During this project, we enhanced our knowledge of Git and collaboration techniques. We were also able to refresh our knowledge of CSS and HTML. Although it was not required, we introduce a small portion of Javascript for the sake of functionality. On the academic side, this project taught us valuable lessons about all the work that went into building the internet infrastructure that we enjoyed today. It started with the core technology that gave us the availability to interconnect computers and then networks of computers. Then we learn about the World Wide Web and Search Engines. Lastly, we concluded our pages with Dotcom Bubble and the impact of social media.
